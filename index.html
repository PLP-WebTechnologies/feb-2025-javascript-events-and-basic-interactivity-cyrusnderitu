Plant Care Guide

Learn how to keep your plants happy, healthy, and thriving.

Most houseplants prefer consistent but moderate watering. Always check soil moisture before watering—overwatering is a common mistake.

Identify your plant’s light needs. Bright, indirect light is ideal for many indoor plants. Avoid placing them in harsh direct sunlight unless specified.

Use well-draining soil suitable for the plant type. Repot annually to refresh nutrients and prevent root binding.

Feed your plant with diluted fertilizer every 4–6 weeks during the growing season (spring and summer). Reduce feeding in winter.
